Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 5, 2026

BoyerScales LLC ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and safeguarded when interacting with our services, websites, and communication systems.

Information We Collect

  • Name, email address, and phone number submitted through website forms
  • Business information submitted during onboarding
  • Customer contact information provided by client businesses for service delivery
  • Service interaction and appointment data
  • Website analytics and technical usage information

How Information Is Used

Information is used solely to provide requested services, including communication handling, appointment scheduling, customer support operations, and automation services performed on behalf of client businesses.

SMS Messaging Notice

By submitting your phone number through a contact or service request form that includes an SMS consent disclosure, you expressly consent to receive SMS messages from BoyerScales LLC in connection with your inquiry. SMS messages are sent only after user-initiated contact — we do not send unsolicited, cold outreach, or promotional SMS messages. Messages are not used for promotional or unsolicited marketing purposes. Users will only receive messages relevant to their specific inquiry or service request.


Message Types. Messages sent may include: responses to your inquiry, follow-up communications related to your request, appointment confirmations or reminders, and customer care communications related to services you have requested.


Message Frequency. Message frequency varies based on your interaction and the nature of your request. You will not receive messages unrelated to your inquiry or service.


Message & Data Rates. Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan.


Opt-Out. You may opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive from us. After opting out, you will receive a single confirmation message and no further SMS messages will be sent.


Help. Reply HELP to any message for assistance, or contact us directly at elijah@boyerscales.com.


No Sharing of Phone Numbers. Phone numbers collected through this website are never sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing purposes. SMS consent obtained by BoyerScales LLC for its own communications is not transferable.


BoyerScales LLC provides messaging systems that are configured to operate only after proper user consent has been obtained. All automated messaging initiated through BoyerScales systems is contingent upon prior express written consent from the end user, in accordance with applicable TCPA and CTIA guidelines.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Information may be processed by trusted service providers solely for delivering contracted services.

Data Protection

We implement administrative, technical, and security safeguards designed to protect personal data from unauthorized access or misuse.
